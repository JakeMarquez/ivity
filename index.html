Available Classes

Wire Wrapping: Basics

Looking to get started in the fine art of jewelry craft? Well pull up your pants and get ready to get your hands dirty, or rather busy, making tons of fun stuff for you and your loved ones. This class will start of doing basic wraps and move on to choosing a stone for a setting, and planning out a piece. No matter your skill level you will be sure to walk out of this class with a strong foundational understanding of the craft, and a keepsake to remember it by.

Wire Wrapping: Advanced

In this class we will be exploring the more advanced topics of wire wrapping. Make sure that you have cleared the Wire Wrapping: Basics course or have auxilary skills equal to what you would have learned in that class. You can always email ahead of time to make sure that you will be prepared for this course. In this class, we will dive deeper into the techniques that make a piece shine, a lot of which i use on my pieces. we will cover:

  • Advanced Wraps
  • Coils
  • Spirals
Additionally, in order to follow along with the demonstrations you will need to pick up a spool of copper wire, and bring a small collection of stones of various sizes to practice on. If you for some reason cannot get these materials, please email ahead to make sure they are available.

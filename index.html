Available Classes

Beginners Wrapping Course

In this course I will teach you some basic terminology, while guiding you through the process of wrapping a cabachon from beginning to end! This course will help you develop the skills necessary to pursue your artistry with wire, and give you a repeatable design that can be applied to many new pendants!

You will need:

  • 1 pair of toothless needle nose pliers
  • 1 pair of flush cutters/end nippers
  • 28g copper round wire
  • 20g Square or round copper wire
  • cabachon to wrap

Price for the course: $20.00

Intermediate Course

In this course we will be expanding upon your skill sets acquired in the previous course! We will be focusing on new concepts, such as coiling and weaving and braiding, as well as important foundational skills that will allow you to increase the complexity of your artwork! With this course, it is expected for you to have a variety of thicknesses and styles of wire to work with, as well as a few additional tools. This course will cover another pendant from beginning to end.

Covered in this course:

  • Framing
  • Coiling
  • Braiding
  • Weaving
  • Layering
  • Discussion on negative space usage
  • Discussion about technique
  • Discussion on art longevity

Price for the course: $50.00

Advanced course

In this course we will be further expanding the skills gained during the previous courses. You will be expected to have a solid understanding of the processes laid out previously. In this course we will go over the skills necessary to truly make your designs stand out!

Covered in this course:

  • Advanced coiling
  • Advanced weaving
  • Facet setting
  • Accent stones
  • 3 dimensional design

Price for the course: $30.00
